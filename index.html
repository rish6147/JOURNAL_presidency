International Journal Of Futuristic Computing and Emerging Technologies

The IJFCET is an open-access, interdisciplinary student journal at the Presidency University , Bangalore. Since 2023, IJFCET’s monthly issues have showcased some of the best academic research works of our undergraduate students across all disciplines of School of Computer Science and Engineering and Information Science. IJFCET is edited and produced by an undergraduate student editorial board of peer editors. Our board is composed of individuals with rich writing, editing, and research experiences who are passionate about the publication of undergraduate work

Origin of the Journal

Presidency University , School of CSE & IS in Bangalore , India publishes the quarterly International Journal of Futuristic Computing and Emerging Technologies. With a present enrollment of over 8500 students, the university offers a wide range of academic programmes, including but not limited to: computer science and engineering, information science , computer applications; management sciences; fundamental sciences; and humanities; and 16 Computer Science and Engineering and Information Science departments. Nearly 500+ people work at various levels of professors at this esteemed university. Presidency University is approved by UGC as State Private University and AICTE- New Delhi for majority of university's undergraduate, post graduate and Ph.D degree courses. The University s Research Publications are heavily influenced by the work of its faculty members, researchers , under graduate and Post Graduate students.

IJFCET was founded in 2023 as an internal publication to serve as a forum for the school of computer science and engineering & information science technological endeavours and for the dissemination of the results of academic and innovations in recent trends in computer science and engineering, information and communication science and technology with campus-based research.

Thanks to the entire team of IJFCET , School of CSE & IS , Presidency University for their untried efforts, the journal going to attain the refereed status in 2023 and the editorial team is working towards receiving ISSN number.

To broaden its readership and welcome submissions from other universities and research facilities, the formerly internal journal became an open access, nationwide publication. From 2023 forward, the journal is proposed to be published four times yearly, once in the months of March, June, September, and December, making it a quarterly publication. Any and all original research and practical applications in the fields of engineering and science are welcome in the journal.

Guidelines to Authors

International Journal of Futuristic Computing and Emerging Technologies (IJFCET)

International Journal of Futuristic Computing and Emerging Technologies (IJFCET) accepts research and review article in Engineering sciences and Technological advances. All manuscripts are subjected to RAPID peer review process. Manuscripts those are original work of author(s) (which are not previously published and are not under consideration for publication by another journal) and having high quality would be published without any delay in subsequent issue. There are no publication charges, Submit Manuscript: Submit your manuscript along with mandatory cover letter by email or submission link. Manuscripts should be prepared as word documents in doc/docx format only. Papers sent in PDF format will not be accepted. A manuscript number will be mailed to the corresponding author within 24 hours. Manuscript needs to be submitted along with Cover letter and Authors declaration form through email attachment at editorijfcet@gmail.com & editorijfcet@presidencyuniversity.in.

ManuScript Categories

International Journal of Futuristic Computing and Emerging Technologies (IJFCET) accepts three types of manuscripts

Review format

i. Full length Research Paper
ii. Short Paper
iii. Review Paper

Note

• Full length research Paper should not generally exceed 25 double-spaced pages of text (not including the references) and should not contain more than 15 figures and/or tables.

• Short Paper should not exceed 10 double-spaced pages of text (not including the references) and no more than 5 figures and/or tables.

• Review Paper should not generally exceed 20 double-spaced pages of text (not including the references) and should not contain more than 10 figures and/or tables

Review Process

All manuscripts are reviewed by the editorial board and assigned reviewers. Decisions will be made as rapidly as possible, and the journal strives to return reviewer’s comments to authors within 3-4 weeks. The editorial board will re-review manuscripts that are pending for revision. It is the goal of the International Journal of Futuristic Computing and Emerging Technologies (IJFCET) to publish accepted manuscripts within 2 weeks after acceptance.

Proofs and reprints:

After acceptance of manuscript for publication electronic proofs will be sent (e-mail attachment) to the corresponding author as a PDF file. Page proofs are considered to be the final version of the manuscript. With the exception of typographical or minor clerical errors, no major changes will be made in the manuscript at the proof stage. Since International Journal of Futuristic Computing and Emerging Technologies (IJFCET) will be published online, authors will have free electronic access to the full text (PDF) of the article. Authors can freely download the PDF file from which they can print unlimited copies of their articles.
